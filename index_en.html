Our massages

Medical Massage

Medical massages are treatments performed on people suffering from musculoskeletal pain. The treatment involves various tools and techniques, combining the worlds of massage and physical therapy, designed to help alleviate pain.

Swedish Massage

The Swedish massage is the most pampering of massages in spas. The treatment combines the use of hands, elbows and forearms designed to stimulate and improve physical and mental health.
The massage helps to improve blood circulation, reduce stress and muscle tension and creates a sense of harmony within the body leading to calmness and peace.

Holistic Massage

This massage helps alleviate pain experienced as a result of mental stress. This massage is most often combined with a Swedish massage and healing oils.

Lymphatic Massage

This massage helps blood and body fluids circulate effectively in the body.

Hot Stones massage

Heated basalt stones are dipped in sesame or hazelnut oils in combination with aromatic oils. You experience is relaxing as the heat helps soothe the stress within your body.
It is the perfect massage for unwinding and relaxing. One of the most requested massages in our spa, creates a treatment that is a liberation and pleasant experience.

Healing Oils

Each oil has its purpose, whether it is to strengthen the immune system or to calm, speed up the blood or suppress anxiety. The combination of the oils in the massage will make all the difference.

Pregnancy Massage

Massaging pregnant women using special treatments relieves pain accumulated as a result of the extra pregnancy weight and prepares the body for a more relaxed birth.
